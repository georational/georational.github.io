Experience 8 artefacts from The British Museum in London in virtual reality.

VRVisitors is a multiuser online exhibition. All visitors to the virtual room will be represented by an avatar,
parameters such as position in space and head rotation are transmitted to and reproduced on every client.

Supports Gear VR and Cardboard. Rift and Vive only on WebVR enabled browsers.

For navigation use touch or game pad or mouse look and  WASD
